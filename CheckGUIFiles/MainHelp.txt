Usage: Click one of the displayed buttons to execute a 
Printer Check or a Runaway check.                                                                                                Selecting 'Printer Check' will open a new window for additional input.                                                                                        Selecting 'Runaway Check' will cause the script to run Immediately and output results into a new window.
